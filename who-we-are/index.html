Who We Are

TASI Test and Automation > Who We Are

TASI Test and Automation is a globally diversified company comprised of three strategic business segments that provide advanced test, inspection, and assembly solutions that improve manufacturing processes and deliver value-added results. Using the latest electronics and software supported by experienced engineering teams, we meet equipment and manufacturing challenges that enable our global customers to achieve the highest quality and reliability in their production environments.

Our solutions range from IIoT enabled products such as real-time process monitoring, to leak test instrumentation and systems, bottle and can inspection, and automated assembly lines.

TASI has served several thousand global customers over the years and enjoys a very high rate of repeat customers, including many Blue-Chip global corporations. Our major markets include automotive, EV, medical devices, HVAC, life sciences, pharmaceutical, packaging, food & beverage, consumer goods, and general industrial equipment. Each TASI business is experienced in delivering world-class products and services, focusing on providing complete engineered solutions to meet the challenges of global customers in these diverse market segments.
